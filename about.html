Our Story

Furniture That
Tells a Story.

FORMA was born from a simple belief: that the objects we live with every day should be beautiful, honest, and made to last. Since 2012, we've been crafting furniture that honours both material and maker.

🪵
The Beginning

From Workshop
to Living Room

FORMA began in a small workshop in Pune in 2012. Founder Vikram Nair, a trained carpenter and design school graduate, was frustrated by furniture that looked good in catalogues but fell apart within years.

He set out to prove that Indian craftsmanship could rival the best in the world — using locally-sourced sustainable hardwoods, traditional joinery techniques, and contemporary Japandi-inspired design.

Today, we operate from a 12,000 sq ft workshop with 40 master craftspeople, and have furnished over 2,400 homes across India.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🎯
Honesty in Materials
No veneer over chipboard. No deceptive finishes. We use what we say we use — solid hardwoods, real metals, genuine upholstery.
♻️
Responsibility to Nature
Every tree used is replaced. We offset 100% of our workshop emissions and use water-based, non-toxic finishes throughout.
🤝
Fair to Our Makers
Our craftspeople earn 40% above industry average. We invest in their training, health, and long-term wellbeing.
